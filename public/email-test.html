Storeverse Authentication

Revamped Verification & Registration Sandbox Interface

Step 1 Initiate Email Verification

Enter your email to request a verification link. A temporary pending account will be created.

Step 2 Simulate/Verify Link

Verify the email token or simulate a browser email link click.

Step 3 Complete Registration

Finalize your profile details. This will succeed ONLY if your email has been verified.

Step 4 Login with OTP (2FA)

Sign in with email and password. A 6-digit code is emailed to you — check the server console for the Ethereal preview link if SMTP is not configured.
